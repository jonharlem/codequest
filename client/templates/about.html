Our Story


We are members of g16 full stack developer program. This project was created to benefit students of Galvanize, as well as job seekers in the software development industry. Our product comprises of a searchable database containing interview questions from well known tech companies and startups alike. Users can search for relevant interview questions pertaining to their position(s) of interest. They can also choose to contribute to the evolving project by entering new interview questions.
